Welcome to the online pet store!
Exotic Shoppette is an all e-commerce business that works with ranches all around the world. We are licenced by the USDA. It is our pleasure to provide you all with an exotic animal. Though, we want to stress how much responsibility it takes to own these precious animals. We are a lot for them and hope you all feel the same way.
Our animals vary from reptiles to aquatics to furry little mammals. We have as follows: Hedgehogs, Chinchillas, Bearded Dragons, Axolotls, and Tarantulas. Our furry, scaly, crawly, and watery friends cannot wait to meet you!
